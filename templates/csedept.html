About the CSE Department

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) aims to provide a strong foundation in computer science theories and practices. Our programs are designed to equip students with cutting-edge technologies, innovation skills, and professional knowledge to thrive in the dynamic IT industry.

Our mission is to deliver quality education, promote research activities, and develop future leaders in the field of computer science and engineering.
